Crochet Keychain Tutorials

Step-by-step guides to help you crochet your own mini keychains. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned hooker, Mie’s tutorials make it fun and easy to create.

Beginner Circle Keychain

A simple round design to learn the basics.

  1. Gather yarn, crochet hook (3–4mm), fiberfill, and a keyring.
  2. Start with a magic ring and crochet 6 single stitches.
  3. Increase each round until you form a small circle.
  4. Stuff lightly with fiberfill.
  5. Close the circle and attach to your keyring.

Mini Bear Keychain

A cute bear face to charm your bag or keys.

  1. Crochet a small circle for the head.
  2. Create two tiny circles for ears and sew them on.
  3. Embroider eyes and nose with thread.
  4. Attach securely to a keyring.

Strawberry Keychain

Bright and cheerful, this strawberry design is fun to make.

  1. Crochet a cone shape with red yarn.
  2. Fill with fiber stuffing.
  3. Add green leaves on top with chain stitches.
  4. Embroider small white seeds.
  5. Attach to your keyring.

Heart Keychain

A sweet little heart to gift or keep.

  1. Chain 6 and form a ring.
  2. Crochet 12 double stitches into the ring.
  3. Shape into a heart by adding increases at the top curves.
  4. Stuff lightly and close.
  5. Attach to your keyring.

Star Keychain

A cheerful star to brighten your day.

  1. Chain 5 and join to form a circle.
  2. Crochet 10 single stitches around.
  3. Create 5 points by chaining and slip-stitching back.
  4. Repeat for each point to form a star.
  5. Attach to your keyring.
